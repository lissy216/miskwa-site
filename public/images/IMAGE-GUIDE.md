# Image Placement Guide

Place the following images in this folder (public/images/). The filenames must match exactly.

## Required Images

1. **red-rain-cover.png** - Red Rain front book cover
   Source: Use your existing Red Rain front cover image

2. **red-rain-bg.jpg** - Red Rain artwork for hero background (dark, atmospheric)
   Source: Use the dark cover art from Red Rain (the shadowy figures/ember artwork)
   Note: This is used at low opacity as a background texture on the shadow side of the split hero

3. **book-of-questions-cover.png** - Book of Questions front cover
   Source: Use your existing Book of Questions cover (the mandala/sun/river design)

4. **book-of-questions-bg.jpg** - Book of Questions artwork for hero background
   Source: Use the river/sun/mandala art from Book of Questions
   Note: Used at low opacity as background on the light side of the split hero

5. **author-portrait.jpg** - Author photo of Miskwa Kimiwan
   Source: Your preferred author photo

6. **all-healed.png** - The golden meditation/ancestral presence artwork
   Source: Use the AllHealed.png image you already have

7. **ltl-program.png** - Living The Light program image
   Source: Use your LTL/Earning the Light program image or the golden witness artwork

## Image Specifications

- Book covers: At least 800px wide, aspect ratio 2:3
- Background images: At least 1920px wide for full-width sections
- Author portrait: At least 800px wide, aspect ratio 3:4
- Program/healing images: At least 800px wide, square aspect ratio preferred

## Optimization

Before deploying, optimize all images:
- Convert to WebP format for smaller file sizes (Next.js Image component handles this automatically)
- Compress JPGs to quality 80-85
- Compress PNGs with tools like TinyPNG
